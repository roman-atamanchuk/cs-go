Business Idea

CS-GO is a student-driven platform that connects students of South East Technological University with small businesses, charities, startups, and community organisations that need digital support.

Instead of spending years creating assignments that are never used in real life, students work on real projects such as websites, mobile apps, databases, online shops, and business systems for real organisations.

This gives students:

* real industry experience,
* stronger CVs and portfolios,
* teamwork and communication skills,
* and better preparation for employment before graduation.

At the same time, small organisations receive affordable digital solutions that help them grow online and become more visible in the modern world.

The platform also includes an AI-powered study system using:

* flashcards,
* mini educational games,
* short TikTok-style learning videos,
* practical problem-solving,
* and integrated AI tools such as ChatGPT.

What makes this idea powerful is that it creates a complete ecosystem connecting:

* students,
* education,
* businesses,
* artificial intelligence,
* and future employers.

As this community grows, the platform can naturally expand into:

* recruitment partnerships,
* premium business services,
* university collaborations,
* AI-powered education tools,
* and sponsored industry programs.

The real long-term value is not only the software platform itself, but the ecosystem, reputation network, and talent pipeline created around it.

CS-GO is designed to become a bridge between education, real industry experience, and future careers.
