Business Idea & Sustainability

CS-GO is a student-driven platform that connects students of South East Technological University with small businesses, charities, churches, startups, and community organisations that need digital support.

The platform solves two major problems:

* students lack real industry experience before graduation,
* small organisations often cannot afford professional software development.

Students work on real projects such as websites, databases, online shops, mobile apps, booking systems, and business tools instead of creating mock assignments that are never used in real life and are eventually thrown into the bin.

This helps students build:

* real experience,
* portfolios,
* teamwork skills,
* professional reputation,
* stronger CVs.

At the same time, organisations receive affordable or free digital solutions that help them grow online.

⸻

Career & Industry Connection

The platform also acts as a bridge between students and industry.

Based on project activity, teamwork, reliability, and reputation, companies can discover talented students for internships, placements, and future employment.

CS-GO creates a centralised ecosystem where businesses can interact with skilled student teams instead of searching for individuals separately.

The platform can also simulate real company workflows and business environments, helping students adapt to modern software development practices before graduation.

⸻

AI-Powered Study System

The platform includes a modern study section focused on fast memorisation and practical learning of all lecture materials related to students’ courses at South East Technological University.

Students learn using:

* flashcards,
* quizzes,
* task-solving approaches,
* educational mini-games,
* TikTok-style short educational videos,
* AI-powered revision systems,
* interactive exercises,
* practical simulations.

Artificial intelligence tools such as ChatGPT￼ can be directly integrated into the platform to help students:

* explain difficult topics,
* generate quizzes and flashcards,
* debug code,
* summarise lectures,
* practice interviews,
* create study plans,
* improve problem-solving skills.

The goal is to make learning faster, more interactive, and more practical.

Business Model & Sustainability

Although the platform starts as a volunteer-based educational ecosystem, it has strong long-term business potential.

Future revenue streams may include:

* premium business services,
* website hosting and maintenance,
* recruitment partnerships with technology companies,
* educational subscriptions,
* sponsored projects,
* grants and innovation funding,
* university partnerships,
* AI-powered premium learning tools.

The real long-term value of the platform is the ecosystem itself:

* student talent,
* business partnerships,
* practical education,
* AI integration,
* real-world project experience,
* industry collaboration.

⸻

Benefits for SETU

The platform can improve the reputation of South East Technological University by demonstrating:

* practical education,
* innovation,
* industry collaboration,
* graduate readiness,
* real student achievements,
* modern AI-supported learning methods.

It shows that students are not only studying theory but building systems that are actually used in real life.

Vision

CS-GO is designed to become a bridge between education, industry, artificial intelligence, and real software development.

The platform helps students become job-ready developers before graduation while helping small organisations grow in the digital world.
